Why donate books?

Giving to charity is a selfless act, thus it will boost your self-esteem and self-worth. Giving money to charity will give you a higher sense of fulfillment and progress since it feels wonderful to serve others and supply them with all of the resources they need.

We are a community aiming to make books available to all passionate readers. We want to help keep the habit of reading alive by giving books away to avid readers at affordable costs. Help us achieve this by becoming a part of our community!
